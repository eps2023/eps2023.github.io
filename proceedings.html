Submission procedure
4-page summaries proceedings of the 49th European Conference On Plasma Physics can now be uploaded here. Please note that due to uncertainties in the publication policy of plasma physics journals following the recent introduction of automated anti-plagiarism checks, EPS-PPD will not make the 4-page papers available immediately. The contributed submissions for this year's conference can be accepted until 31 July.
Please respect the following strong naming convention used for the Program and Poster sessions agenda : epsplasma2023.eu/ For example, the first talk on Monday after Alfven prize is: I1.002_Schneider.pdf The first poster on Tuesday is: Tu_MCF1_Baschetti.pdf



** Warning ** Any file with a different naming convention won't be accepted..
